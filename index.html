About Me

Thanks for visiting my portfolio site! Here's some information about who I am:

I spent five years at the University of Wisconsin double majoring in Biology and Jazz Studies, and graduated in 2017 with a Bachelor of Science. After I realized sometime around my last semester that neither of my majors were what I wanted to pursue as my career, I decided to try teaching myself to code. After spending a year teaching myself Swift and loving every minute, I've decided I want to make coding my career!

To that end, I'm participating in the University of Minnesota's full time Coding Bootcamp this fall. I'll learn a bunch of cool stuff including Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, JSON, AJAX, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Java, and MERN. After that's finished, I hope to become a web developer or software engineer in the Twin Cities area. As a lifelong learner and lover of collaborative coding, I can't wait to find my first full-time coding job. If I sound like the kind of developer you'd want in your company, feel free to contact me with questions, or just hire me!

Seriously, please hire me.
